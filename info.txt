PL/SQL Engine cannot do any DML or DDL operations. It passes these operations to SQL Engine.
%TYPE attribute gets the datatype of the referenced column or value & assigns that datatype to the declared variable.
The maximum length of the variable name can be 30 characters.
What is difference between CASE statement &  case expression?: The result of a CASE expression is a single value, whereas the result of a CASE statement is the execution of a sequence of PL/SQL statements.
